The Four Noble Truths

  1. Life involves suffering (Dukkha).
  2. Suffering has a cause — craving.
  3. Ending craving ends suffering.
  4. The Eightfold Path is the way to end suffering.

The Noble Eightfold Path

  • Right Understanding
  • Right Thought
  • Right Speech
  • Right Action
  • Right Livelihood
  • Right Effort
  • Right Mindfulness
  • Right Concentration
